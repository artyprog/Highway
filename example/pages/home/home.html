HighwayJS

Thank you for using Highway JS! One of the simplest and quickest routing libraries for Javascript!

Why use HighwayJS?

There are many reasons why you should use HighwayJS in your web apps! Here are a few:

  • Make a web application with no browser reloading.
  • Light Weight! Only 4Kb!
  • Incredibly fast! Loads all pages and is completely ready in milliseconds!
  • No Dependencies! None at all!
  • Use with popular frameworks or no framework.
